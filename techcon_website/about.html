Our History

TechCon began in 2005 as a small meetup for passionate developers. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated global tech conferences.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, inclusion, and knowledge sharing in the tech community. We aim to empower the next generation of developers, creators, and leaders.
